Bio

Zac Lopez-Ibanez is a designer and creative entrepreneur. His design work spans a variety of media including furniture, graphic and video design as well residential architecture. Zac will receive his Bachelor of Arts in New Media from UNCA in the Fall of 2020. There, his concentration in interactive media and video art have been underpinned by his course work in computer science and business. In addition, he pursued a degree at the Savannah College of Art and Design for 2 Â½ years, with a focus on product design, prior to finishing his degree at the University of North Carolina.

His work has appeared in national publications including Dwell Design, The L.A. Times, and Carolina Home + Garden. He has delivered lectures at Rhode Island School of Design (RISD) as well as Appalachian State University in Design and Industrial Design practices, and was awarded the best in show award from Dwell on Design in 2015.

Zac is currently working as a product designer for the creative design firm Anthroware in Asheville, NC. Here, he specializes in brand development, UX/UI design, and business strategy in an effort to help other creative entrepreneurs succeed. Zac lives in Asheville, North Carolina with his wife, two children, a curious bunny, and their faithful dog Silo.
