About

Surreal food for the artist in everyone.

Café Magritte was founded by chef and entrepreneur Antoine Garnier in 2004 with long time firend and partner Zac Lopez-Ibanez. Credited with “the rise of contemporary French-American cuisine” by the New York Times and named the “most important café in America” by Bon Appétit magazine, Café Magrittehas opened its restaurant in the heart of Asheville, NC. Their first café has gained world-wide recognition for their innovative take on farm to table french cusine, while supporting sustainable and responsible farmers and food purveyors.
